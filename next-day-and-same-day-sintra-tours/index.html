Choose Which Attractions in Sintra to Prioritise

With so much to see in Sintra, the surrounding countryside and the coast, it's almost impossible to fit it all into half a day, or even a full day. That's where our expertise comes in. We can help you to really make the most of the time you do have available, to suggest the best, most efficient route, and to offer compromises where necessary.

Explore the Coast - The Portuguese Riviera

Cabo da Roca, Boca do Inferno, Cascais, Estoril - explore the world-famous Portuguese Riviera which connects Lisbon with Sintra.

As well visiting the world famous landmarks of Sintra, why not also visit the most westerly point of mainland Europe - Cabo da Roca, just down the road from Sintra. Stunning views and a typically windy cliff top make this a must see for tours en route back to Lisbon.
