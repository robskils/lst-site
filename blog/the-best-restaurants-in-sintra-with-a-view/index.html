The Best Restaurants in Sintra with a View

Sintra offers a variety of top restaurants with breathtaking views. Whether you're craving seafood at Búzio, indulging in steak at Sal & Xixa, or enjoying Mediterranean flavors at Bar do Fundo, these dining spots combine delicious cuisine with scenic landscapes. Perfect for both romantic and family outings, these venues provide memorable experiences.

Located amidst Portugal’s verdant landscapes, Sintra beckons people with its timeless allure. This picturesque town, known for its romantic charm and historical significance, enchants those who wander through its streets and gardens. With all its wonders, from Moorish fortresses to imposing palaces, Sintra stands as a testament to the centuries of culture that have shaped its identity. Its beauty is only matched by the diversity of its offerings - from the mist that often shrouds its hills to the delicate flavors that grace its dining tables. Sintra invites its visitors to enter a world where nature and heritage, in harmony, create an experience that lingers in the heart long after the journey’s end.

Amidst the lush landscapes and romantic charm of Sintra, we can find a collection of great restaurants. As the Sun sets on the city, these establishments elevate the experience by allowing you to savor the gorgeous landscapes and their fantastic food. Whether you’re a discerning gastronome seeking culinary delights or a wanderer seeking natural beauty, any of these restaurants will give you a good taste of the national cuisine. Embark on a culinary journey through Sintra’s finest dining establishments and prepare to be amazed.

Búzio

Near one of Portugal’s most renowned beaches, Praia das Maçãs, Búzio is the restaurant for seafood enthusiasts. A haven for lovers of fish, shellfish, and lobster, this venue offers an array of ocean treasures. Complementing its menu is an exceptional wine cellar, ensuring a marriage of flavors that will leave you asking for more. The restaurant’s doors are open seven days a week, welcoming guests from noon until 10 pm. With an impressive score of 9.3/10 on The Fork, Búzio stands as a force to be reckoned with, especially for those seeking seafood by the seaside.

Sal & Xixa Steakhouse

For those whose palates favor meat, Sal&XiXa Steakhouse presents an alternative. This establishment, also situated near Praia das Maçãs, specializes in crafting delicious meat dishes, national and international. A perfect choice for families and groups, the doors remain open from 6 pm to midnight, seven days a week. Garnering a stellar 9.4/10 rating on The Fork, Sal&XiXa Steakhouse secures its place among the region’s finest establishments.

Dona Azeitona

In the charming enclave of Arneiro dos Marinheiros, near Magoito Beach, Dona Azeitona is the place to visit for pizza aficionados. Besides its amazing pizzas, this pizzeria also serves a selection of delicious pasta dishes, such as tagliatelle carbonara, Bolognese, and lasagna. Praised for its “welcoming, tranquil, and delicious ambiance”, Dona Azeitona invites guests to taste its culinary creations from Tuesday to Sunday, at both lunch and dinner hours. With a noteworthy 9.3/10, it solidifies its reputation as one of the best restaurants with a view.

Bar do Fundo

Taste a variety of Mediterranean flavors at Bar do Fundo, a venue renowned for its diverse menu, ranging from delicious soups to succulent meat and fish dishes. Located by Praia Grande, this beachside restaurant exudes an ambiance perfect for romantic rendezvous, especially at sunset and when the ambient lights cast a romantic glow after dusk. Operating seven days a week, from 12:30 pm to 10 pm, Bar do Fundo boasts an admirable 9.2/10 on The Fork, making it perfect for indulging in a symphony of tastes against the backdrop of the shoreline.

Terrace - Hotel Arribas

Mirroring the Mediterranean specialty of the previous entry is Terrace, nestled within Hotel Arribas on the opposite side of Praia Grande. This restaurant boasts a diverse array of dishes, both of national and international origin, offering à la carte and buffet options. As you taste your delicious meal, a gorgeous view of the beachfront enhances your dining experience. With operating hours aligning with lunch and dinner, Terrace is an ideal venue for relaxation, events, and business meetings, boasting an impressive score of 9.2/10 on The Fork.

Arola

With an impressive score of 9/10 on the Fork, we have the restaurant, Arola. Located in Penha Longa, they’re known for a variety of dishes, ranging from tapas to delicious meat dishes. As you eat, you’ll be amazed by the beautiful landscapes, including the local golf course and the mountain where wonders such as the Pena Palace and the Moorish Castle are located. Being the perfect place to organize business meetings or events for holidays such as Easter or Christmas, they’re open seven days a week, from noon to 10:30 pm, closing earlier on Tuesdays and Wednesdays.

Experience Enhanced by Comfort and Hospitality

Rest assured that in these establishments, you’re never far from the comfort of the English language, as the staff members are required to speak it. Most of these venues also provide free Wi-Fi and air-conditioning, ensuring your comfort whilst you indulge in your meal. Additionally, they offer the convenience of private parking for their clients. The Portuguese are quite hospitable, so if you need help figuring something out, feel free to ask. We have a reputation for being “human GPS’s,” you can be sure you’ll be aided.

A Culinary Odyssey Awaits

Let your senses run wild and embark on a journey through Sintra’s restaurants. As you enjoy your meals, and as you take on the beautiful landscapes of Sintra, you will create memories that will linger long after the last bite. Discover the magic of Sintra's finest restaurants, where culinary ability and breathtaking landscapes unite to create a masterpiece that's as delectable as it is mesmerizing. Your heart and palate will thank you for this experience.
