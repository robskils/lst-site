Tour Information

Where do we meet the tour?

The tour will be fully private. Therefore we can collect you from your hotel or Airbnb in Lisbon, Cascais or Sintra. We will also drop you back off at the end of the day. 

What's not included?

  • Lunch
  • Gratuity - we recommend a tip of €50 for the guide if you are satisfied with the day they have given you. 

Will the Guide Accompany Us to the Attractions?

For car tours and people carriers, typically up to 8 people, whether the guide will accompany you into the attraction will depend on the parking conditions, as they will need to look after the vehicle. Wherever it is not possible for them to come in with you , they will brief you before entrance. There is lots of information available at each of the attractions, including audio tours if you would like. 
For larger groups where there is a dedicated driver, the guide will accompany you into the attractions. Please liaise with your booking manager in advance to ensure that the guide has tickets for the same entrance time as your group.

If you are a smaller group, and would like the guide to accompany you into the attractions, we will need a separate driver, so there will be an additional charge. However it is very possible.
