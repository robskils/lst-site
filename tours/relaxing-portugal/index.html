OVERVIEW

Discover Portugal: A Relaxing 3-Day Tour for Seniors

Experience the beauty, culture, and charm of Portugal on a thoughtfully designed 3-day tour created with seniors in mind. With a relaxed pace and handpicked attractions, this journey showcases Portugal’s highlights while ensuring comfort and ease.

Day 1: Lisbon’s Charm and Culture

Begin your journey in Lisbon, where the cobbled streets and historic charm await. Visit the iconic Belém Tower and Jerónimos Monastery, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Enjoy a serene stroll through Eduardo VII Park and take in the panoramic views of the city. End the day with a leisurely riverside dinner, savouring traditional Portuguese cuisine.

Day 2: Sintra and Cascais – Enchanting Escapes

Venture to the fairytale town of Sintra, famous for its romantic palaces and lush gardens. Explore the majestic Pena Palace and take a gentle walk through the Quinta da Regaleira gardens. Afterward, enjoy a coastal drive to Cascais, a charming seaside town. Relax with a coffee overlooking the sparkling Atlantic Ocean before returning to Lisbon.

Day 3: Fátima and Óbidos – Tranquility and Tradition

On your final day, visit the Sanctuary of Fátima, a serene pilgrimage site offering moments of peace and reflection. Continue to Óbidos, a medieval village with enchanting cobbled streets, whitewashed houses, and vibrant bougainvillea. Stroll at your own pace and savor the region’s signature cherry liqueur, ginjinha. Return to Lisbon with unforgettable memories of Portugal’s history and charm.

This 3-day tour balances captivating sights with plenty of downtime, offering seniors an enriching yet comfortable travel experience. Book now and let Portugal’s timeless allure leave you inspired!

Where do we meet the tour?

The tour will be fully private. Therefore we can collect you from your hotel or Airbnb in Lisbon, Cascais or Sintra. We will also drop you back off at the end of the day. 

What's not included?

  • Lunch
  • Gratuity - we recommend a tip of €50 for the guide if you are satisfied with the day they have given you. 

Will the Guide Accompany Us to the Attractions?

For car tours and people carriers, typically up to 8 people, whether the guide will accompany you into the attraction will depend on the parking conditions, as they will need to look after the vehicle. Wherever it is not possible for them to come in with you , they will brief you before entrance. There is lots of information available at each of the attractions, including audio tours if you would like. 
For larger groups where there is a dedicated driver, the guide will accompany you into the attractions. Please liaise with your booking manager in advance to ensure that the guide has tickets for the same entrance time as your group.

If you are a smaller group, and would like the guide to accompany you into the attractions, we will need a separate driver, so there will be an additional charge. However it is very possible.
