Porto and Aveiro

2 Day Tour from Lisbon

OVERVIEW

Embark on a captivating two-day journey from Lisbon to Porto and Aveiro, exploring some of Portugal's most iconic destinations. Start your adventure early in Lisbon and travel north along scenic routes. Arrive in Porto, a UNESCO World Heritage city known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. Visit the Ribeira district with its colorful facades, enjoy the views of the Dom Luís I Bridge, and take a relaxing cruise on the Douro River. Don’t miss a visit to a port wine cellar in Vila Nova de Gaia for a guided tasting of Portugal's renowned fortified wine. 

After lunch at a traditional restaurant, explore landmarks such as the Clérigos Tower, São Bento Railway Station with its famous azulejo tiles, and Livraria Lello, one of the world’s most beautiful bookstores. Spend the night in Porto, enjoying its lively atmosphere.

On day two, head to Aveiro, often called the "Venice of Portugal." Stroll along its charming canals, where brightly painted moliceiro boats glide through the water. Visit the fish market and sample local delicacies like ovos moles, a sweet treat unique to the region. Explore Art Nouveau architecture, or rent a bike to discover the picturesque salt pans and Costa Nova’s striped beach houses nearby.

In the afternoon, begin your return to Lisbon, stopping along the way to enjoy scenic views or quaint villages. Reflect on the cultural richness and natural beauty experienced over these two days. The journey from Lisbon to Porto and Aveiro offers a perfect mix of history, gastronomy, and coastal charm, ensuring unforgettable memories and a deeper appreciation for Portugal's diverse landscapes and traditions.

AGENDA

Two-Day Tour Agenda: Lisbon → Porto → Aveiro → Lisbon
Day 1: Lisbon to Porto

  • Morning: Depart Lisbon, travel to Porto.
  • Late morning: Explore Ribeira district, Douro River, and Dom Luís I Bridge.
  • Afternoon: Visit a port wine cellar in Vila Nova de Gaia with wine tasting.
  • Evening: See Clérigos Tower, São Bento Railway Station, and Livraria Lello.
  • Overnight: Stay in Porto.
     

Day 2: Porto to Aveiro and Return

  • Morning: Travel to Aveiro.
  • Explore canals, moliceiro boat rides, and Art Nouveau architecture.
  • Taste local specialties like ovos moles.
  • Afternoon: Visit Costa Nova beach houses or salt pans.
  • Evening: Return to Lisbon, reflecting on the journey.

Will the Guide Accompany Us to the Attractions?

For car tours and people carriers, typically up to 8 people, whether the guide will accompany you into the attraction will depend on the parking conditions, as they will need to look after the vehicle. Wherever it is not possible for them to come in with you , they will brief you before entrance. There is lots of information available at each of the attractions, including audio tours if you would like. 
For larger groups where there is a dedicated driver, the guide will accompany you into the attractions. Please liaise with your booking manager in advance to ensure that the guide has tickets for the same entrance time as your group.

If you are a smaller group, and would like the guide to accompany you into the attractions, we will need a separate driver, so there will be an additional charge. However it is very possible.
