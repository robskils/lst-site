The Best Hotels in Lisbon

This article highlights some of the best hotels in Lisbon for travellers seeking luxury, comfort, or unique experiences. It features recommendations such as Art Nouveau-inspired Hotel 1908 Lisboa, combining historical charm with modern amenities, and hotels along the picturesque Tagus riverside. These accommodations offer convenient access to Lisbon's vibrant neighbourhoods, cultural landmarks, and waterfront attractions, ensuring a memorable stay in Portugal's capital.

Lisbon, Portugal’s vibrant capital, is a city that seamlessly blends old-world charm with modern innovation. Nestled between hills and the Tagus River, it offers visitors a rich cultural experience, exceptional cuisine, and some of Europe’s most picturesque landscapes. For those planning to visit this dynamic city, choosing the right hotel can elevate your trip from good to unforgettable. Whether you're seeking luxury, comfort, or a boutique stay, Lisbon offers a wide variety of accommodations to suit all tastes and budgets. In this guide, we’ll explore the best hotels in Lisbon that provide exceptional service, stunning locations, and a unique Portuguese experience.

Bairro Alto Hotel – A Luxe Boutique Experience

Nestled in the heart of Lisbon's iconic Bairro Alto neighbourhood, the Bairro Alto Hotel offers an elegant blend of contemporary design and historical charm. Known for its stunning rooftop terrace with panoramic views of the city, the Bairro Alto Hotel is perfect for those who want to experience Lisbon’s nightlife, culture, and history all in one place. The rooms here are spacious, beautifully designed, and outfitted with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay.

Bairro Alto itself is renowned for its narrow streets, lively bars, and vibrant atmosphere. This hotel’s location puts you within walking distance of Lisbon’s best attractions, including the historic Alfama district, the iconic tram 28, and the chic shopping streets of Avenida da Liberdade. It’s a great base for exploring Lisbon while offering the tranquility of a luxury retreat in the bustling city.

Why Stay Here: Rooftop bar with breathtaking views, luxurious interiors, excellent service, and a prime location in Bairro Alto.

Four Seasons Hotel Ritz Lisbon – Opulence and Tradition

If you’re looking for a truly luxurious experience, the Four Seasons Hotel Ritz Lisbon is an iconic establishment that offers both opulence and traditional Portuguese charm. Located on the Avenida da Liberdade, this five-star hotel features sophisticated rooms with grand views of the Eduardo VII Park, the Tagus River, and the historic city center. The hotel is an architectural gem, with a neoclassical façade and lavish interiors that exude both grandeur and comfort.

Guests can unwind at the Ritz Spa, enjoy a swim in the outdoor pool, or dine at the sophisticated Varanda restaurant, which serves gourmet Portuguese cuisine. The hotel’s service is second to none, offering a high-end experience that will make you feel like royalty. Its central location means that guests can easily visit nearby attractions like the Gulbenkian Museum, the historic Belém Tower, and the charming Chiado neighbourhood.

Why Stay Here: Impeccable service, luxurious amenities, stunning views, and a central location near Lisbon's best cultural and shopping districts.

Altis Belem Hotel & Spa – A Modern Riverside Escape

For those who prefer a modern, riverside experience, the Altis Belem Hotel & Spa offers a sleek and stylish retreat along the Tagus River. Located in the Belém district, famous for its historical landmarks like the Jerónimos Monastery and the Tower of Belém, this hotel is perfect for travelers who enjoy the combination of contemporary design and rich history.

The Altis Belem Hotel features spacious rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows, offering views of the river or the garden. The hotel also boasts a full-service spa, an outdoor pool, and the Michelin-starred Feitoria restaurant, where guests can indulge in gourmet cuisine inspired by Portuguese flavors. The combination of luxury and location makes it an excellent choice for visitors looking to experience Lisbon’s culture and beauty from a tranquil riverside setting.

Why Stay Here: Modern design, riverside location, excellent dining options, and proximity to Lisbon’s most famous historical sites.

The Vintage Lisboa Hotel – A Charming Blend of Style and Comfort

If you're seeking a stylish yet affordable hotel in Lisbon, the Vintage Lisboa Hotel offers a perfect balance of comfort and design. Situated in the lively Bairro Alto neighborhood, this boutique hotel combines contemporary elements with classic Portuguese touches, creating a unique ambiance that feels both chic and cozy.

The Vintage Lisboa Hotel is known for its friendly and professional staff, who go out of their way to ensure guests have a memorable stay. The hotel also features an inviting outdoor pool, a rooftop bar with stunning views, and a delicious breakfast spread. Guests can easily walk to attractions like the nearby Chiado district, the lively Baixa area, and the scenic Miradouro de Santa Catarina, offering fantastic views of the city.

Why Stay Here: Affordable luxury, stylish interiors, central location, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Pestana Palace Lisboa Hotel & National Monument – A Historic Stay

For those who wish to experience the grandeur of a historic palace, the Pestana Palace Lisboa Hotel & National Monument is an excellent choice. Situated in a beautiful 19th-century palace, the hotel offers guests an immersive experience of old-world luxury combined with modern amenities. The hotel is set within lush gardens and boasts an outdoor swimming pool, a wellness center, and a renowned fine-dining restaurant.

The rooms at Pestana Palace are spacious and elegantly furnished, some offering views of the surrounding gardens or the Tagus River. Guests can take a leisurely stroll through the gardens, visit nearby monuments, or take a short drive into the city center for sightseeing. The hotel's location is perfect for travellers who want to stay in a tranquil and historic setting without sacrificing proximity to Lisbon’s top attractions.

Why Stay Here: Historic charm, beautiful gardens, outdoor pool, and fine-dining options, with easy access to Lisbon’s major attractions.

Lisboa Carmo Hotel – A Blend of Tradition and Modernity

The Lisboa Carmo Hotel, located in the Baixa district, offers a unique fusion of old-world charm and contemporary design. This boutique hotel is housed in a beautifully restored building, retaining many of the traditional features of Lisbon’s historic architecture, while incorporating sleek, modern furnishings and amenities.

The hotel is known for its excellent service, offering guests personalized attention to make their stay truly memorable. The rooms are stylish, and some offer stunning views of the Santa Justa Lift and the historic Carmo Convent. Guests can enjoy a delicious breakfast in the cozy dining room, and the hotel’s central location means that many of Lisbon’s famous attractions, including the Alfama district and the Praça do Comércio, are just a short walk away.

Why Stay Here: Excellent location in the Baixa district, stylish design, and close to major Lisbon attractions.

Hotel Avenida Palace – A Luxurious, Timeless Classic

For a classic and sophisticated stay, the Hotel Avenida Palace is one of Lisbon’s finest traditional hotels. Situated on the grand Avenida da Liberdade, this five-star hotel has been a symbol of elegance since its opening in the early 20th century. The hotel’s neoclassical architecture, chandeliers, and antique furniture exude old-world charm, while the modern amenities ensure that guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Hotel Avenida Palace is renowned for its exceptional service, and guests often return for the attention to detail and welcoming atmosphere. The hotel is within walking distance of major shopping streets, the cultural hub of Avenida da Liberdade, and Lisbon’s historic neighborhoods. Its proximity to public transport, such as the metro and tram, makes it a great base for those looking to explore the city.

Why Stay Here: Classic luxury, historic charm, excellent service, and a prime location near Lisbon's top attractions.

LX Boutique Hotel – A Stylish, Hip Stay in Cais do Sodré

For a trendy, modern hotel experience, look no further than the LX Boutique Hotel in the Cais do Sodré district. This area has recently become one of Lisbon’s most fashionable neighborhoods, with a wide array of cool bars, cafes, and shops. The LX Boutique Hotel fits right in, offering chic rooms with contemporary design and vibrant accents that reflect the city’s youthful energy.

The hotel is housed in a restored 18th-century building, blending Lisbon’s historic charm with modern touches. Its location is ideal for those who want to explore the vibrant nightlife of Cais do Sodré or take a short walk to the famous Mercado da Ribeira. The hotel offers a cozy bar, a welcoming lounge area, and a delicious breakfast, making it the perfect place for those seeking a blend of style and convenience.

Why Stay Here: Trendy design, lively neighborhood, great service, and proximity to Lisbon's vibrant nightlife and dining scene.

Santiago de Alfama – A Boutique Stay with Historic Charm

Located in the heart of the historic Alfama district, Santiago de Alfama is a small, boutique hotel that offers an intimate and personal experience. The hotel’s design is inspired by the traditional architectural elements of Lisbon, with a contemporary twist. The rooms are cozy, with a mix of modern furnishings and traditional Portuguese touches, and many offer beautiful views of the winding streets of Alfama.

Santiago de Alfama’s location is perfect for those who want to explore the narrow, picturesque streets of this UNESCO World Heritage site. Guests can easily walk to the iconic São Jorge Castle, the Lisbon Cathedral, and the Miradouro de Santa Luzia, which offers one of the best viewpoints in the city. The hotel’s breakfast is highly praised, and the staff’s attention to detail ensures that guests feel at home during their stay.

Why Stay Here: Historic charm, intimate atmosphere, excellent service, and a central location in Alfama.

Lisbon offers a diverse array of accommodation options that cater to different tastes, budgets, and travel styles. From luxurious, five-star properties with stunning views to stylish boutique hotels in historic neighbourhoods, the city has something for everyone. No matter where you stay, Lisbon’s unique atmosphere and charm are sure to leave a lasting impression.
