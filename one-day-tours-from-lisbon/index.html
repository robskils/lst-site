Royal Sintra

Our full day tours really pack in as much as you possibly can in one day. Typically we start by driving to Sintra. We will pick you up from your accommodation wherever it is in Lisbon, Cascais or Sintra. We like to start promptly at 9am.

The morning in Sintra typically includes a trip to the Pena National Palace and Park followed by lunch in Sintra. Lunch in Sintra also affords a great opportunity to have a look around the old town.

Depending on how the morning went, we tend to visit one or two palaces or gardens after lunch in Sintra.

And then head out of the town to pick up some additional sites on the coast such as Cabo da Roca, the most Westerly point of mainland Europe, Cascais and Estoril, Carcavelos, and even Ericeira if that is a priority for you.

FAQ

How can I book a tour with Lisbon Sintra Tours?

You can book a tour by visiting our website and filling out the booking form or by contacting our customer service team via email or phone.

Can I customize the tour itinerary?

Yes, we offer personalized tours and can tailor the itinerary according to your preferences and interests. Just let us know your requirements, and we will create a customized tour for you.

Are the entrance fees included in the tour price?

The entrance fees to attractions are not included in the tour price unless specified. However, our tour guides can assist you in purchasing tickets or provide recommendations for attractions to visit.

What languages do your tour guides speak?

Our tour guides are fluent in English, but we also offer tours in other languages such as Spanish, French, and Portuguese. Please let us know your language preference when booking a tour.

Lisbon Sintra Tours specialises in bespoke private tours throughout Portugal, offering personalised itineraries, expert tour guides, and a range of cultural experiences.
