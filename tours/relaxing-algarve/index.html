OVERVIEW

Relaxing Algarve Tour: 3-Day Itinerary

Escape to Portugal's stunning Algarve region for three days of relaxation and laid-back exploration. This itinerary is designed for those who want a serene and unhurried getaway, combining coastal beauty, local culture, and delicious cuisine.

Day 1: Arrival and Coastal Relaxation

 

  • Morning: Depart from Lisbon or arrive directly in the Algarve. Settle into your accommodation in a charming coastal town like Lagos, Albufeira, or Vilamoura.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the local beach or unwind by the hotel pool.
  • Evening: Savor a relaxed dinner of fresh seafood at a beachfront restaurant, taking in the sunset over the ocean.

Day 2: Gentle Exploration and Scenic Views
 

  • Morning: Visit a nearby coastal town such as Lagos or Alvor. Explore the quaint streets, local shops, and historical landmarks like the Fortress of Sagres or the Church of St. Anthony.
  • Afternoon: Take a gentle boat trip along the coast to admire the stunning cliffs and caves, including the famous Benagil Cave. Alternatively, relax on a quiet beach or enjoy a spa session at your hotel.
  • Evening: Relish a casual dinner in a traditional Algarvian restaurant, trying dishes like cataplana or grilled sardines.
     

Day 3: Leisurely Farewell

 

  • Morning: Begin the day with a relaxed breakfast and a walk along a scenic beach or nature trail, such as Ria Formosa Natural Park.
  • Afternoon: Spend time at leisure—perhaps indulge in some last-minute shopping for local crafts or relax with a coffee in a seaside café.
  • Evening: Depart from the Algarve, feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.
     

This 3-day Algarve tour offers the perfect balance of relaxation and light exploration, ensuring a rejuvenating break in one of Portugal’s most picturesque regions.

Tour Information

Where do we meet the tour?

The tour will be fully private. Therefore we can collect you from your hotel or Airbnb in Lisbon, Cascais or Sintra. We will also drop you back off at the end of the day. 

What's not included?

  • Lunch
  • Gratuity - we recommend a tip of €50 for the guide if you are satisfied with the day they have given you. 

Will the Guide Accompany Us to the Attractions?

For car tours and people carriers, typically up to 8 people, whether the guide will accompany you into the attraction will depend on the parking conditions, as they will need to look after the vehicle. Wherever it is not possible for them to come in with you , they will brief you before entrance. There is lots of information available at each of the attractions, including audio tours if you would like. 
For larger groups where there is a dedicated driver, the guide will accompany you into the attractions. Please liaise with your booking manager in advance to ensure that the guide has tickets for the same entrance time as your group.

If you are a smaller group, and would like the guide to accompany you into the attractions, we will need a separate driver, so there will be an additional charge. However it is very possible.
